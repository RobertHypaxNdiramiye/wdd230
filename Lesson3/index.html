Virunga Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to grow business relationships between business owners, entrepreneurs, tourists, and customers around the region of Virunga National Park which is located in the north province of Rwanda. The website provides information for business owners and community members. The site also provides opportunities to connect with other local business owners, meeting schedules, and other relevant information.
