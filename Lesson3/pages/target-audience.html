Target Audience

The target audience for the Virunga Chamber of Commerce site includes business owners, entrepreneurs, potential business owners, and those interested in the commerce and tourism sector of the Virunga National Park, in the north province of Rwanda. The target audience also includes anyone looking for information about businesses in the area or looking to help local businesses thrive.

Personas

Eldon

Eldon is a business owner who runs a motel inn in Virunga. His motel includes 20 employees and is very busy in the summer season as well as winter, when Gorillas are more likely to be encountered.

Eldon attended a local education institution that providen him with skills in Hotel Management and tourism. He learned how to run a a motel by applying himself in jobs of the same niche until he had gathered enough money to begin his business..

Eldon is 35 years old and has a wife and two daughters. When he's not working he enjoys spending time in the national volcanic park of Virunga.

Eldon is concerned about the increasing prices of car parts and the difficulty in acquiring parts important to the success of his business. He's also worried about growing his business as demand increases with increased tourism and new growth in the town.

Neema

Neema is a 25-year-old living in the Virunga region, she recently started a small busines of a souvenir store in the small town of Ruhengeri near Virunga Natinal Park.

Neema has no degree. She began the business using the money she enherited from her late parents, she has decided to keep serving the community of her town where her parents taughter everything she knows about handcrafted souvenirs.

Neema doesn't know a lot about running a business but she's a creative problem solver. She's most concerned with getting regular customers and acquiring new, different, and attractive items which is a hard challenge given that she has no connection with others who knows how to produce handmade souvenir items.
